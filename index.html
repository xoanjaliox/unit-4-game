C R Y S T A L     C O L L E C T O R

H O W     T O     P L A Y:

A random target number will be given at the start of the game.

Click on one of the four crystals being shown to add to your score.

Values of the crystals won't be known until the crystal has been clicked on.

Match your score with the random target number given at the start to win.

If your score goes over the random target number, you lose.

Values of the crystals will change when the game is restarted.
